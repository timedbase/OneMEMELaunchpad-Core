Factory — Instant Actions

Creation Fee

Flat BNB fee charged at token launch. Sent to the feeRecipient.

Default Bonding-Curve Params

Virtual BNB seed and migration target applied to new launches. Managers may also update.

Add Manager

Managers may call setDefaultParams only.

Remove Manager

Revoke manager role from an address.

Check Manager

Query whether an address has the manager role on the factory.

Propose Ownership Transfer

Two-step. New owner must call acceptOwnership(). Not reversible once accepted.

Accept Ownership

You are the pending owner. Call acceptOwnership() to complete the transfer.

Cancel Timelock Action

Cancel any queued but not yet executed timelock action by its action ID (bytes32).

Rescue Stray BNB

Sweep BNB from the BondingCurve that exceeds the sum of all active pool balances (e.g. direct sends).

Platform Fee

Charity Fee

BPS sent to charityWallet on every trade.

Fee Recipient

Charity Wallet

EOA receiving the charity split. Zero address redirects to feeRecipient.
Not queued
