Creation Fee

Flat fee charged in BNB when launching a new token.

Default Bonding-Curve Params

Virtual BNB seed and BNB raise target for new launches. Managers may also update this.

Platform Fee

48h Timelock
BPS sent to feeRecipient on each trade. Combined with charity fee, max total 2.5%.
Not queued

Charity Fee

48h Timelock
BPS sent to charityWallet on each trade.
Not queued

Fee Recipient

48h Timelock
EOA that receives the platform portion of trade fees.
Not queued

Charity Wallet

48h Timelock
EOA that receives the charity portion. Set to zero address to redirect to feeRecipient.
Not queued

PancakeSwap Router

48h Timelock
Router used for future migrations only. Existing tokens snapshot the router at creation.
Not queued

Cancel Timelock Action

Cancel any queued but not yet executed timelock action by its action ID (bytes32).

Accept Ownership
